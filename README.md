# Kallol Movement  
A renaissance reconnecting Muslim Rajputs with their ancient Sanatani Rajput lineage.  
Created by **Kallol Chakrabarti (Kallol), Independent Researcher**

The Kallol Movement celebrates shared ancestry, valor, and cultural depth. It brings together research, genealogy tools, identity planners, and a modern governance model to help Muslim Rajputs explore their layered heritage with clarity and confidence.

---

## Vision
The movement encourages a return to roots through ancestry research, cultural bridges, and practical tools. It supports harmony across faiths and borders by focusing on heritage, not labels.

**Core themes:**
- Shared Rajput ancestry  
- Interfaith harmony  
- Cultural pride  
- Evidence-based genealogy  
- Collaborative leadership
