Kallol Movement

A renaissance reconnecting Muslim Rajputs with their ancient Sanatani Rajput lineage.

About the Movement

The Kallol Movement explores shared heritage, valor, and identity across Rajput communities. It encourages cultural pride, interfaith harmony, and evidence-based ancestry research. The initiative is led by Kallol Chakrabarti (Kallol), an independent researcher.

Rajput Roots Navigator (Ready Reckoner)

This toolkit helps Muslim Rajputs understand and verify their ancestral lineage. It brings together genealogy tools, cultural reference tables, and identity planners into one easy guide.

Key Features

  • Genealogy Quick-Check with clan timelines and conversion-era insights
  • Simple heritage-compatibility calculator based on user input
  • Cultural and legal reckoner for traditions and rights in India/Pakistan
  • Step-by-step identity and heritage integration roadmap
  • Templates for family trees and oral history documentation
  • Gamified artifact scanner (upcoming)

Helix Governance for MRSS

The Helix model brings a modern leadership structure inspired by two intertwined strands:

Capability Helix

  • Skill building and mentorship
  • Genealogy and heritage training
  • Talent marketplace connecting members to roles

Value-Creation Helix

  • Action-oriented project teams
  • Event management and outreach
  • Heritage advocacy and community initiatives

Together, these streams help integrate Muslim Rajputs into MRSS work while encouraging balanced participation and collaboration.

Roadmap
